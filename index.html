BlackJack Game

GamePlay

1. Click on the "Hit" button and notice the Score [You:0] will increase everytime.

2. You have to click repeatedly on "Hit" button and carefully set a score of ⩽ 21 to win the Game. If you cross 21 then you will 'burst' the score.

3. Then click on the "Stand" button & wait till *Bot/Dealer* play all it's turns.

4. Now you can notice the result on the top and the score table on the bottom will be updated.

5. Next, Click on the "Deal" button. It will reset the Current Gameplay but the score table will be the same.

6. By following the upper instructions you can play again n again and try to beat the *Bot/Dealer*.

Have FUN :)
